A life in the ascendant, one full of vitality, exuberance and cheerfulness, is supported by and partially expressed through a strong, energetic and beautiful body. It is the duty of an iron hermit to cultivate both his mental and physical strength to the maximum. The neglect of physical fitness and beauty among many contemporary thinkers is perhaps a symptom (or is it the cause?) of a society in decadence.

Beginner

This is a variation of Stronglifts 5x5, augmented with a few auxiliary lifts. I highly recommend this to those new to strength training. A strong strength base is helpful regardless of your fitness goals.

Monday Tuesday Thursday Friday
Squat 5x5 Squat 5x5 Squat 5x5 Overhead Press 5x5
Bench Press 5x5 Overhead Press 5x5 Bench Press 5x5 Deadlift 5x5
Barbell Row 5x5 Deadlift 1x5 Barbell Row 5x5 Core Exercises
Dips 3x8 Lateral Raise 3x8 Cable Row 3x8 Calf Raise 3x8

Intermediate & Advanced

This is a variation of Power Hypertrophy Upper Lower (PHUL). This 4-day split aims to strike a balance between strength and hypertrophy. It's quite a heavy plan so make sure you get plenty of sleep and food for recovery.

Monday Tuesday
Barbell Bench 5x5 Deadlift 4x5
Flye 3x8 Squat 4x5
Barbell Row 3x5 Leg Press 3x12
Overhead Press 3x8 Calf Raise 3x12
Lat Pulldown 3x8 Plank 3x1 min
Dips 3x8
Tricep Pushdown 3x8

Thursday Friday
Dumbbell Bench 3x8 Squat 3x10
Lateral Raise 3x12 Jumping Squat 2x10
Front Raise 3x12 Leg Press 3x10
Cable Row 3x10 Leg Curl 3x10
Tricep Pushdown 3x10 Static Hold 3x2min
Bicep Curl 3x10 Plank 3x1min
